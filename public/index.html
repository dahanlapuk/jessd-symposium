About Us

Journal of Environmental Science and Sustainable Development is pleased to announce the 5th International Symposium (Virtual) of Earth, Energy, Environmental Science and Sustainable Development 2024, which will be held at the Central of the Capital City, Jakarta, on December 6th-7th, 2024, with the main theme “Environment and Sustainable Future”. This event aims to be the leading annual conference in the research field related to earth, energy, environmental science, and sustainable development. We encourage researchers, scientists, intellectuals, and professionals worldwide to present their advanced research for a better environment.
